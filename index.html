Data Viz Prototypes

Prototyping data visualizations that are more accessible to people with disabilities. This page experiments with approaches for creating web-based data visualizations, that are more accessible to people who are blind or visually impaired.

Prototype 1

A bar chart visualization of the population of the fictional country Loremland. It shows how many people there are in each age group in the Loremland population.

Prototype 2

A line chart visualization of the birth and death rate in the fictional country Loremland. It shows the number of births and deaths by year from 1950 to 2021.
