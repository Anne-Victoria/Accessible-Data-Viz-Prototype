Prototype 1 — Population of Germany by age (2018)

Usage: Use tab or mouse hover over the bars to see the tooltips of the individual data points. Use the escape key or move the mouse away from the bar to close the tooltip. When using a screen reader, use the tab key to access the individual data points in the visualization.

Skip to sonification

A bar chart showing the age distribution of the population in Germany in 2018. Ages are grouped into 20 groups starting with 0 to 4 and ending with 95 to 99. 100 and over and not depicted. Age groups are on the x axis. Number of people in the age group is on the y axis. Y axis ranges from 0 to 7,000,000. Maximum at 50 to 54 years with 6,875,000 people. Minimum at 95 to 99 with 144,000 people. Average population per group is 4,149,950.

Sonification

Listen to the data. Use the button to play and pause the sonification. The sonification plays one sound for each population group along the x axis, starting with 0–4 and ending with the 95–99. Higher-pitched sounds represent a larger population size in that group. Lower-pitched sounds represent a smaller size.

Data Source

The data was taken from Statistisches Bundesamt and is licensed under the Data licence Germany - attribution - Version 2.0. The data has been modified in the following way: it was aggregated into groups spanning five years of age, and it was visualized.
