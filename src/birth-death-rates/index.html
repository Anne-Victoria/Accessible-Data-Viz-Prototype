Prototype 2 — Yearly total number of births and deaths in the fictional country Loremland from 1950 to 2021

Usage: Use tab or mouse hover over the lines to see the tooltips of the individual data points. Use the escape key or move the mouse away from the the visualization to close the tooltip. When using a screen reader, use the tab key to access the individual data points in the visualization.

Skip to sonification

A line chart showing the number of births and deaths per year in Loremland. X axis shows the year from 1950 to 2021. Y axis shows the absolute number of births and of deaths in that year from 0 to 1,400,000. One line represents births. Maximum at 1,357,304 in 1964. Minimum at 662,685 in 2011. Average 906,972. Second line represents deaths. Maximum at 1,023,687 in 2021. Minimum at 748,329 in 1950. Average 890,155.

The number of births gradually increases, then abruptly drops until 1973. Afterwards it slightly fluctuates on a lower level. The number of deaths slightly increases at first, then slightly decreases, then slightly increases again. The number of deaths starts out much lower than the number of births, but first surpasses it in 1972 during the steep drop of births. After that it stays above the number of births until the end.

Sonification

Listen to the data. Use the radio buttons to choose which data series to play. Use the button to play and pause the sonification. The sonification plays one or two sounds for each data point across the x axis, starting with the year 1950 and ending with 2021. Higher-pitched sounds represent higher numbers of births or deaths. Lower-pitched sounds represent lower numbers. The number of sounds per datapoint depends on whether you choose to listen to one data series or both at the same time.
